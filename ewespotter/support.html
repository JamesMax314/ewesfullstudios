General Instructions

Welcome to the Ewespotter support page. Here you can find general instructions on how to use our services.

Getting Started

To begin using Ewespotter, please follow these steps:

  1. Select a photo from your phone library or take a new photo
  2. Adjust the photo to centre the sheep in the middle and ensure that the image is appropriately oriented
  3. Click the right arrow to run the image through model
  4. Wait for the model to process the image
  5. View the results displayed on the screen and name your sheep
  6. You can now save the sheep to the flock

For every sheep saved to the flock, you will recieve an additional pixel sheep on the home page.
