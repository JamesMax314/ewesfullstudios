Effective Date: 05/07/2025

Thank you for using our app.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or images. All processing—including any image-based machine learning tasks—is performed entirely on your device.

2. Image Processing

The app may request access to your device’s camera or photo library in order to perform its functions. However, all image analysis happens locally and no images are uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, the app is safe for use by individuals of all ages. However, we recommend parental supervision for children, as with all digital tools.

5. Security

Because we do not transmit or store data, your information remains entirely on your device, enhancing your security and privacy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
