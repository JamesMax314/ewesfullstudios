Newe Spotter - General Instructions

Welcome to the Newe Spotter support page. Here you can find general instructions on how to use our services.

Getting Started

To begin using Newe Spotter, please follow these steps:

  1. Select a photo from your phone library or take a new photo.
  2. Adjust the photo to centre the sheep and ensure the image is properly oriented.
  3. Click the right arrow to run the image through the model.
  4. Wait for the model to process the image.
  5. View the results displayed on the screen and name your sheep.
  6. You can now save the sheep to the flock.

For every sheep saved to the flock, you will receive an additional pixel sheep on the home page.
